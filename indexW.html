Pocket Currency Converter

A pocket app that allows you to easily transfer currency for your travels.

When I traveled to India, I often found myself unsure if I was paying too much because I did not know the local conversion rates. This app helps you quickly compare prices abroad to familiar items back home, so you can avoid confusion or getting overcharged. I have expanded it to include multiple currencies, making it easy for travelers everywhere! Just click the flags to select your currencies and see how far your money really goes. (Sound on!)
